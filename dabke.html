Dabke

What is it?

The dabke is a traditional Lebanese (and Middle Eastern) line or circle dance frequently performed at weddings.

We'd like to give it a try at our wedding, and we'd like you to participate. The basic steps are pretty simple, so please take a look at the videos below. We're looking forward to dancing with you!
